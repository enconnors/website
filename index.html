Elliot Connors is a Canadian-American writer based in Brooklyn, New York.

He is currently a playwright-in-residence at the theater company Colt Coeur and a semifinalist for the Humanitas Carol Mendelsohn Award.

His essays have been featured in The Best American Essays, McSweeney’s and The Yale Record, of which he was Editor in Chief.

As a writer’s assistant, he has punched up features for Sony, Netflix, and Amazon.

His debut short film will premiere on NoBudge in August.

MFA: NYU. BA: Yale.
