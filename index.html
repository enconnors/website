Elliot Connors is a Brooklyn-based writer for the stage, screen, and internet.

His work has been featured in The Best American Essays, McSweeney’s Childrearing anthology, and The Yale Record, the world’s oldest humor magazine, where he served as Editor in Chief.

He is an MFA candidate at NYU Tisch.
