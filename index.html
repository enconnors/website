About Contact Thanks

Elliot is a Canadian-American writer based in Brooklyn.

He is a 2026 Just for Laughs “New Face” for his adult animated series OMNI, about a dystopian virtual reality and the group of lovable curmudgeons trying to change it.

He’s also been a playwright-in-residence at the theater company Colt Coeur and shortlisted for awards from Humanitas, Rattlestick Theater, and Anarchists United.

As a writer’s assistant, he has punched up features for Sony, Netflix, and Amazon.

His prose has been featured in The Best American Essays, McSweeney’s, and The Yale Record, of which he was Editor-in-Chief.

His debut short film will premiere on NoBudge August 4.

MFA: NYU. BA: Yale.
