Elliot Connors
Elliot Connors is a Canadian-American writer for the stage and screen. He is currently a playwright-in-residence at the NYC theater company Colt Coeur and a semifinalist for the Humanitas Carol Mendelsohn Award. His prose has been featured in The Best American Essays, McSweeney’s and The Yale Record, of which he was Editor in Chief. As a writer’s assistant, he has punched up features for Sony, Netflix, and Amazon. His debut short film will premiere on NoBudge in August. MFA: NYU, BA: Yale.
