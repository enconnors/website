ELLIOTWEB

My Graphs Made the News

1.
My graphs made
the news.
Girlfriend’s excited
but people’s graphs make
the news
all the time, and besides,
I think my graphs
might be wrong.

2.
Shouldn’t have told
anyone my graphs
made the news I’m
really not excited about
it. Girlfriend says
I should put them on
my resume but where
would they even go?
Leadership?

3.
My boss tells me I’m in
line for a pay raise and I
can’t help but wonder
if it’s because my
graphs made the news.
But I don’t make
graphs for the
money or the
fame I make
graphs because
I’m a masochist.
If I were my boss
I would’ve said,
“You want a pay raise?
People make graphs
better than you.
People make graphs
faster than you.
Your dad has a bigger dick
than you.
Graph that,
bitch.”

4.
Can’t find my
poetry notebook and
I’m worried because
some of my most
valuable poems
are in there.
I was just telling
my friend
I don’t know
if my poems
are any good but
they’re sure worth a lot
of money.
He wanted to buy this
poem on the spot
but I gave it
to charity.

5.
You would
tell me if
something I
said was
a poem, right?
I ask my girlfriend.
I’m as paranoid
about losing
a poem as I am
about losing my
poetry notebook.
This thing has
some of my
most valuable poems
in it. Really need to
scan them.

6.
I told my girlfriend
I wish
I could give you
half the poems
that plague me in
such abundance and
she got offended and
asked if I
thought she had no
talent of her own.
And I was like what?
My poem net
worth is 2 million dollars.
If I gave you
half my poems
you’d be worth 1 million
dollars. That’s almost
as much as
your mom
makes in a year.

7.
I said something and
my girlfriend was like
That’s a poem.
And I was like no,
that was a skit.
If skits were
poems, John
Belushi would
be our Nobel
Laureate and
Louise Gluck
would be our
president.

8.
I google
“Louise Gluck graphs”
but she has
only poems
in the news.
Sucker.
