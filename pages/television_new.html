The author in his Halloween costume.

Elliot Connors has written the following for the stage and screen:

Seed

When an ambitious young VC associate gets pregnant, she’s forced to lean on her eccentric group of friends in New York.

Metaverse

A recently divorced Mark Zuckerberg learns to love again in the Metaverse.

Bethesda

Two families with sordid pasts vie for control of a cultish D.C. suburb and the eclectic private school at its center.

Whose Life

A directionless 20-something is bequeathed his dead friend’s life, leading him on an absurd journey to discover himself through the lives of others.

ILOF

A self-obsessed obituary writer realizes he can kill people to write more viral obituaries and jumpstart his literary career, only to find that his brilliant, reclusive mother is orchestrating a far more insidious plot in their small, seaside town.

HIMYM

Marshall and Barney accompany Lily and her kindergarten class to a Vietnam War Reenactment. Robin and Ted help Ted's grandfather reunite with his "Double Reverse Miss Saigon."

Sketches

A man rants about political correctness at the McDonald's drive-thru. The milkman comes, goes, is gone. An improv troupe struggles to get a suggestion other than "stallions".
