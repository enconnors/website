Elliot Connors

Poor Zuckerberg

Wake up at 4
thinking about Facebook
and I want to kill myself.
Turn towards the window
and think about jumping.
That would shut everybody the fuck up.

Nobody likes me and
I don’t give a fuck.
Kind of cared when
they made Social Network
but now I just think it’s funny.
Everybody has such a short
attention span. Remember
Cambridge Analytica?
Something new to talk about
on the app I fucking invented.
Like mice on coke
running around the maze
yelling about how
shitty the coke is.

Everyone’s so stupid this
app’s so dumb. At least
Congress is talking about this shit
instead of another Iraq.
More people
would kill themselves
if Facebook didn’t exist,
probably.
Kids always find a way
to get bullied or depressed.
I just monetized it.

Check my phone.
No texts, no one
texts me anymore,
everyone thinks I’m too busy,
which is fine. I don’t really
want to talk to anyone.
Spend all my time
thinking about how
everyone talks to
everyone else
simultaneously and
instantaneously.
How am I supposed to find
any one conversation
interesting?
It’s like those guys
who watch so much porn
they can’t come from sex,
speaking of which,
I haven’t come in months.
Sex is boring
and I can’t focus
long enough to masturbate.
So many things more interesting
than coming. So many things more
interesting than people.

Sometimes wake up
feeling so shitty
I slouch over to Priscilla’s room
and crawl into her bed
and nuzzle her, except she’s
so cold. I kiss her neck
while she fingers herself.
I’ll never learn anything new.
I’m smarter than everyone
and the algorithms are smarter
than me, but sure,
I’ll fix them so there’s
less hate speech.
Fine. Whatever. Anything else?
Perhaps an algorithm
to make your wife come?
