Elliot Connors

The author with his father on Facetime.

Elliot Connors is a writer from Rochester, New York.

His latest television pilot - about an obituary writer who starts killing people to write more interesting obituaries and jumpstart his literary career - was a quarterfinalist for the 2021 Screencraft Pilot competition.

His latest play is about a recently divorced Mark Zuckerberg who finds love again in the Metaverse.

His essays have been honored or featured in the The Best American Essays of 2021, The Adroit Journal and the Yale Literary Magazine.

His comedy has been published in McSweeney's Internet Tendency and the Yale Record, of which he was Editor in Chief in 2018.
